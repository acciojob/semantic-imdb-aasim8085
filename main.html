Summary

*Inception* is a sci-fi thriller that follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from people's subconscious during dreams. He's offered a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can perform an impossible task: **inception**—planting an idea into someone's mind. Cobb assembles a team to navigate dream layers and complete the mission.
